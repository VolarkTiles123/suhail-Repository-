# UAE LOCATIONS & MARKET GUIDE

## Primary Markets (Tier 1)

### Sharjah
- **Home Base** - Maliha Road, Industrial Area 18
- **Population** - 1.6M+
- **Market Size** - Large (local primary)
- **Key Demographics** - Families, contractors, developers
- **Growth Rate** - Steady (real estate development)
- **Service Radius** - 30km to Dubai, 50km to Abu Dhabi

### Dubai
- **Population** - 3.6M+
- **Market Size** - Largest UAE market
- **Key Demographics** - Affluent, expat, luxury focus
- **Growth Rate** - High (continuous development)
- **Service Area** - Northern emirates to Abu Dhabi
- **Opportunity** - Highest volume market

## Secondary Markets (Tier 2)

### Abu Dhabi
- **Population** - 1.5M+
- **Market Size** - Large (capital, government projects)
- **Key Demographics** - Government, commercial, residential
- **Growth Rate** - Moderate (large projects)
- **Distance** - 140km south from Sharjah
- **Opportunity** - High-value projects

### Ajman
- **Population** - 400K+
- **Market Size** - Medium (growing residential)
- **Key Demographics** - Residential, middle-income
- **Growth Rate** - Growing (new developments)
- **Distance** - 30km north of Sharjah
- **Opportunity** - Untapped market segment

## Extended Markets (Tier 3)

### Ras Al Khaimah (RAK)
- **Population** - 350K+
- **Distance** - 80km north of Sharjah
- **Opportunity** - Emerging market

### Fujairah
- **Population** - 150K+
- **Distance** - 120km northeast of Sharjah
- **Opportunity** - Small niche market

### Umm Al Quwain
- **Population** - 80K+
- **Distance** - 60km north of Sharjah
- **Opportunity** - Minimal (small population)

## Market Dynamics

### Dubai Market
- Highest competition
- Largest volume opportunity
- Premium pricing accepted
- Fast-moving market
- Expat-focused marketing effective

### Sharjah Market
- Base location advantage
- Strong local community
- Growing middle-class
- Price-sensitive segment
- Local networking important

### Abu Dhabi Market
- Government/commercial focus
- Large project opportunities
- Premium quality required
- Longer sales cycles
- Relationship-based buying

### Ajman/Northern Markets
- Emerging opportunity
- Price-sensitive
- Less competition
- Growing residential construction
- Underserved market
