# TILE SIZES & SPECIFICATIONS

## Standard Sizes (Most Common)

### Small Format
- **60×60 cm** - Traditional square, versatile
- **60×120 cm** - Rectangular, modern look
- **80×80 cm** - Large square, contemporary

### Large Format
- **120×120 cm** - Premium square, luxury feel
- **120×240 cm** - Rectangular large, dramatic
- **120×280 cm** - Extra large rectangular
- **160×320 cm** - XL format, statement piece

## Thickness Options

Standard thickness levels:
- **6mm** - Thin, lightweight
- **9mm** - Standard residential
- **12mm** - Heavy duty commercial
- **15mm** - Extra durable industrial
- **20mm** - Premium thick format

## Size Selection Guide

### Bathroom
- **Small spaces:** 60×60, 60×120
- **Standard:** 80×80, 120×120
- **Luxury:** 120×240, 120×280

### Kitchen
- **Workarea:** 60×60 (traditional)
- **Floors:** 120×120, 120×240 (easy to clean)
- **Backsplash:** 30×60, 60×120

### Living Rooms
- **Modern:** 120×120, 120×240
- **Contemporary:** 160×320 (feature wall)
- **Traditional:** 80×80, 60×120

### Outdoor
- **Large format:** 120×240, 160×320
- **Anti-slip:** 120×120 (textured)
- **Durability:** 12-15mm thickness

## Color & Finish

### Available Finishes
- **Gloss** - Reflective, elegant, requires maintenance
- **Matte** - Modern, low-glare, forgiving
- **Textured** - Grip, outdoor-friendly
- **Polished** - Luxury, slippery (indoor only)

### Color Options
- **Whites & Creams** - Bright, spacious feeling
- **Grays & Blacks** - Modern, sophisticated
- **Earth Tones** - Warm, natural feeling
- **Blues & Greens** - Trendy, calming
- **Decorative Patterns** - Feature walls, artistic

## Porcelain vs Ceramic

| Spec | Porcelain | Ceramic |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Water Absorption | <0.5% | 3-10% |
| Durability | Very High | Moderate |
| Application | All | Indoor |
| Cost | Higher | Lower |
| Thickness | Variable | 10-15mm |
| Maintenance | Low | Low |
