## Backlink Analysis Strategy

### Where Should We Get Links?

**1. Architecture/Interior Design Blogs**
   - Topic: Tile selection, design trends
   - Link type: Mentions, case studies, guest posts
   - Value: HIGH (relevant, authoritative)
   - Example: Architecture magazine features

**2. Construction/Real Estate Sites**
   - Topic: Building materials, specifications
   - Link type: Supplier directories, case studies
   - Value: MEDIUM (relevant, variable authority)
   - Example: Construction portal listings

**3. UAE Business Directories**
   - Topic: Local business listings
   - Link type: Business profile, local directory
   - Value: MEDIUM (local authority, contextual)
   - Example: Sharjah Chamber of Commerce

**4. Home Improvement Portals**
   - Topic: DIY guides, product reviews
   - Link type: Quotes, citations, guides
   - Value: MEDIUM (traffic, authority variable)
   - Example: Home improvement blogs

**5. Industry Associations**
   - Topic: Material standards, certifications
   - Link type: Member directory, certifications
   - Value: HIGH (authority, relevance)
   - Example: Building Materials Association

### Competitor Backlink Audit

**What we find:**
1. Backlinks to competitor site

4. Relevance to tiles/construction

## Keyword Opportunity Matrix

**Competitive Position:**

```
         HIGH VOLUME
              ▲
              │    Defend (Own These)
              │
Medium       │    Win (Target These)
Difficulty  │
              │    Avoid (Too Hard)
              ▼
         LOW VOLUME
         LOW←---DIFFICULTY---→HIGH
```

**Strategy:**
- **Defend:** Keywords where we rank top 3 (maintain, improve)
- **Win:** Keywords with 50+ volume, < 30 difficulty, we rank 6-30
- **Avoid:** Keywords with > 50 difficulty (usually not worth effort)
- **Monitor:** Emerging keywords, trending topics, seasonal spikes
