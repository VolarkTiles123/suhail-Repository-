## Backlink Building Process

### 1. Prospect Research
```
Criteria:
- Domain Rating (DR) 30+
- Relevant to tiles/construction/design
- Active (recent updates)
- Linkable content (not news only)
- Contact information available
```

### 2. Outreach Email Template
```
Subject: Guest Post Opportunity - [Topic] for [Blog Name]

Hi [Name],

We've been following your excellent content on [specific article].
We'd love to contribute a guide on [related topic] for your audience.

Suggested title: "[Compelling title related to their content]"

Why it works for your readers: [Clear benefit]

We're Volark Tiles, a leading supplier in the UAE with 
expertise in tile selection, design, and installation.

Best,
[Name]
[Contact info]
```

### 3. Follow-up
- Initial outreach
- Wait 7 days
- Follow-up #1 (friendly reminder)
- Wait 7 days
- Follow-up #2 (last attempt)

### 4. Tracking
```
Domain | DR | Contact | Status | Link Type | Date Acquired
------|----|---------| -------|-----------|---------------
[site] | 45 | Email   | PITCH  | Guest post| -
[site] | 38 | Form    | PENDING| Mention  | -
[site] | 52 | Direct  | LINKED | Citation | 2024-01-15
```
