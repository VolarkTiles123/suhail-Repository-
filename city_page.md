## Tips for City Pages

✅ Make it personal (local experience shows)
✅ Show local expertise (projects, testimonials)
✅ Be specific (delivery times, local solutions)
✅ Address local concerns (climate, architecture style)
✅ Include city pride (why they love city)
❌ Don't be generic (avoid copy-paste feel)
❌ Don't over-promise (realistic delivery/service)
