## Content Standards

### EEAT Requirements
- **Expertise** - Product knowledge, tile specifications
- **Experience** - Real customer stories, case studies
- **Authoritativeness** - Written by tile specialist (Suhail Ahmed Gour)
- **Trustworthiness** - Verified business, customer reviews, transparent pricing

### Content Format Requirements
- Mobile-first (80%+ UAE traffic is mobile)
- Minimum 1000-1500 words for pillar content
- 300-600 words for cluster content
- Include images (optimized, <100KB)
- Schema markup required (Product, FAQ, LocalBusiness)
- Internal linking (2-4 per piece)
- Bilingual where possible (English + Arabic)

### Tone of Voice
- Professional but approachable
- Educational first (help customers decide)
- Sales-focused second (guide to products)
- Local UAE context
- No hyperbole or false claims
